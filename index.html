Project Overview

Integrated Township

Reliance Model Economic Township (MET) is an integrated industrial, residential, and commercial township spread across 8,250 acres in Jhajjar district, Haryana.

Developer

Developed by Reliance Ventures Ltd. (subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited).

Vision

Aim: Create a self-sustained smart city with industries, housing, schools, hospitals, and recreation.

Government Approval

Approved by the Government of Haryana under the Industrial Model Township framework.

Reliance MET City Project Location Benefits

National Highway Connectivity

Directly connected to national highways (NH-2, NH-8, NH-71, NH-10) in NCR via the Kundli Manesar Palwal Expressway

State Highway Access

The Jhajjar-Farukhnagar-Gurgaon State Highway (SH 15 A) four-lane median divided road runs across the western border of the MET site.

Major District Roads

Two major district roads (MDRs), viz Jhajjar-Badli-Delhi Road (MDR123) and Bahdurgarh-Badli-Chandu Road(MDR136) are four-laned and pass along the Northern and Eastern parts of the MET respectively.

Airport Connectivity

Quick access to IGI Airport in Delhi via the SH-15A and MDR-136 or Dwarka Expressway.

KMP Expressway

Additional Details About Reliance Met City Jhajjar

Prime Location

Reliance MET City Residential Plots are located on the main Gurgaon Jhajjar State Highway 15-A (Gurgaon Jhajjar Road) near Farukhnagar, Haryana.

Water Supply Infrastructure

Features a dedicated water supply system sourced from the NCR Canal. The water undergoes treatment in a modern facility before being distributed through a specialized pipeline network. With an approved allocation of 100 MLD and an 18 km water pipeline, along with essential components like a Water Treatment Plant, Master Balancing Reservoirs, and a SCADA-controlled Distribution System, ensuring reliable water supply.

Government Support and Incentives

Receives strong government support through tax incentives and subsidies, fostering investment and growth while creating employment opportunities and enhancing living standards. As a key player in the "Make in India" initiative, contributing to the nation's development strategy.

Key Infrastructure Benefits

The drainage and sewerage system is meticulously planned to manage wastewater and sewage, integrating sewage and effluent treatment plants for environmentally responsible disposal. Communication is streamlined through a reliable high-speed system, including fiber optic connectivity and robust telecom networks. A robust safety and security framework ensures well-being with CCTV surveillance and round-the-clock security measures.

Reliable Power Infrastructure

Boasts a robust power supply infrastructure, ensuring uninterrupted electricity to industrial units. This dedicated network offers reliable and cost-effective power, designed to meet the needs of industries in the MET. Utilizing supercritical technology, the power sub-station is both efficient and eco-friendly, connected to the national grid for a dependable electricity source.
